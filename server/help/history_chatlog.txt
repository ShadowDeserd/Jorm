Title: History, chatlog
Syntax: "history [log option]" 

Options: chat, say, shout, combat

You can look at the last 20 messages sent with the history command.
You can filter the history with the options above like so:
 "history shout" - this will display the last 20 shouts (including yours)

You can use more than one setting:
 "history say combat" - this will display the last 20 combat and say messages
